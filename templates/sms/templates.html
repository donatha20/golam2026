Available SMS Templates
Loan Approval

Template: loan_approval.txt
Usage: Sent when a loan is approved

Sample Message:
"Congratulations John Doe! Your loan application LN001 for ₹50,000 has been approved at 12% for 24 months. Please visit our office for disbursement. - Golam Financial"
Variables: borrower_name, loan_number, loan_amount, interest_rate, tenure
Payment Reminder

Template: payment_reminder.txt
Usage: Sent to remind borrowers of upcoming payments

Sample Message:
"Dear John Doe, your loan payment of ₹5,000 for loan LN001 is due on Jan 15, 2024. Please make payment to avoid late charges. - Golam Financial"
Variables: borrower_name, outstanding_amount, loan_number, next_due_date
Overdue Reminder

Template: overdue_reminder.txt
Usage: Sent for overdue loan payments

Sample Message:
"URGENT: Dear John Doe, your loan LN001 payment of ₹5,000 is 5 days overdue. Please contact us immediately or make payment to avoid further action. - Golam Financial"
Variables: borrower_name, loan_number, outstanding_amount, days_overdue
Payment Confirmation

Template: payment_confirmation.txt
Usage: Sent after successful payment

Sample Message:
"Thank you John Doe! Payment of ₹5,000 for loan LN001 received on Jan 15, 2024. Remaining balance: ₹45,000. Ref: PAY001. - Golam Financial"
Variables: borrower_name, amount, loan_number, payment_date, remaining_balance
Loan Disbursement

Template: loan_disbursement.txt
Usage: Sent when loan amount is disbursed

Sample Message:
"Dear John Doe, your loan LN001 amount of ₹50,000 has been disbursed on Jan 10, 2024. First payment due: Jan 25, 2024. - Golam Financial"
Variables: borrower_name, loan_number, disbursed_amount, disbursement_date
Savings Transaction

Template: savings_transaction.txt
Usage: Sent for savings account transactions

Sample Message:
"Dear John Doe, Deposit of ₹1,000 to account SAV001 successful on Jan 15, 2024. Current balance: ₹15,000. - Golam Financial"
Variables: borrower_name, transaction_type, amount, account_number, balance
Template Guidelines
Template Variables
  • {{ "{{ borrower_name }}" }} - Full name of the borrower
  • {{ "{{ loan_number }}" }} - Loan reference number
  • {{ "{{ amount }}" }} - Transaction amount
  • {{ "{{ organization_name }}" }} - Your organization name
Best Practices
  • Keep messages under 160 characters when possible
  • Always include organization name
  • Use clear, professional language
  • Include relevant reference numbers
