About Accounting Periods

Accounting periods help organize your financial data by time periods for reporting and analysis.

  • Open: Transactions can be posted to this period
  • Closed: No new transactions, but can be reopened
  • Locked: Permanently closed, cannot be modified
  • Current Period: The active period for new transactions
