Calculation Methodology

Retained Earnings Formula:

Retained Earnings = (Total Income - Total Expenditure) - Capital Withdrawals
  • Total Income: All recorded income from various sources
  • Total Expenditure: Only approved and completed expenditures
  • Capital Withdrawals: Distributions to shareholders/owners
  • Profit Margin: (Net Income ÷ Total Income) × 100
  • Return on Equity: (Net Income ÷ Total Capital Investment) × 100
